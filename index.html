Hi, this is Chunyue, and welcome to my site!

I am deeply passionate about working with and continually learning about distributed software systems. One of my key interests lies in exploring the availability and resilience of large scale software projects. I am fascinated by how such projects, despite their scale and complexity, maintain their functionality and continue to deliver high performance. This includes considering how these systems can withstand high loads, handle errors efficiently, and recover from failures swiftly. Additionally, I also enjoy delving into the concept of the observability of distributed systems. I am intrigued by the process of monitoring and understanding the state of the system based on the outputs it produces. Specifically, can we truly understand what is happening in the production such that we can always respond in the most accurate and fast way possible?
